Our Learning Philosophy

We replace arbitrary word memorization with structured orthographic mastery, spaced repetition, and pop-culture storytelling.

🧠

Spaced Repetition & Rules

Rather than forcing rote memorization, RewardSpeller focuses on underlying orthographic rules (vowel teams, affixes, syllable division). Words are introduced through structured scaffolding, transitioning from visual copying to pure working memory recall.

🧙‍♂️

Immersive Themed Learning

To keep 8-to-12-year-olds deeply engaged, every single lesson and vocabulary definition is wrapped in rich fantasy and pop-culture lore drawing heavily from Minecraft, Star Wars, Harry Potter, The Lord of the Rings, and Narnia.

🎧

Neural Audio Dictation

Our platform utilizes high-fidelity Google Cloud Neural Text-to-Speech narration to deliver pristine, natural-sounding dictation. Students develop strong phonetic awareness by connecting visual text directly to professional audio pronunciation.

State Standards Curriculum

Meticulously calibrated across 3rd through 6th-grade state educational benchmarks to combat the summer slide and ensure academic readiness.

📚

3rd & 4th Grade Standards

Focuses on foundational vowel teams, complex vowel patterns (I before E, Bossy R, Magic E), basic affixes (un-, re-, -ful, -less), compound words, syllable division rules (VC/CV, Schwa sounds), and introductory math/science academic vocabulary.

🏛️

5th & 6th Grade Standards

Advances into complex diphthongs & trigraphs (ough, au, aw), intermediate Latin roots (spect, tract), advanced Greek roots (bio, geo, photo), assimilated prefixes (im-, suf-, com-), syllable stress shifts, and high-school prep vocabulary.

⚔️

Final Boss Mastery Assessments

At the end of each Tier, a randomized 10-word "Final Boss" assessment evaluates true long-term retention. Students must demonstrate 90%+ accuracy across constituent sections before advancing to higher curriculum tiers.

Parent-Funded Rewards Economy

Bridging the gap between daily spelling practice and real-world motivation through a fully customizable parental command center.

💎

Earn Base Points & Streaks

Students earn points for every correctly spelled word on their first attempt. Points scale dramatically with difficulty (Easy [1pt] → Medium [3pts] → Hard [30pts]), with consecutive correct answers stacking a streak bonus up to a 2.0x multiplier!

🎁

Custom Rewards Vault

Parents define customized real-world incentives inside the Parent Portal (e.g., 30 mins Video Game Time, Trip to the Park, Pizza Night). As students earn points, progress bars fill up toward their coveted goals.

🏆

Milestone Achievements

Our non-deducting rewards economy ensures claiming a reward never subtracts points from a student's cumulative hard-earned balance. Once achieved, rewards turn emerald green and display a permanent badge of honor.
